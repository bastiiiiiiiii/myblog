Places Must Visit

crimson boracay

i had visited in dec 2018

To get to Boracay you must take a plane to Caticlan airport and then take a boat to Boracay. Once to park in the dock the staff will welcome you witht some instrumental music and take you to your booked rooom which may be a suite or a villa. In some resorts before you enter your room they will give you a tropical drink or a hot towel. The resort offers you fre service to explore the other islands besides Boracay. When you wake up in the morning you may either go to the gym or the spa! There are many children facilities for kids such as: art rooms, board games, and even a pirate ship playground. Before you leave you can get souvenirs in the souvenirs shop.

Dubai

i had visited in dec 2022

In Dubai you can do almost practically anything. First you may go to the dubai mall which will have: kidszania, Lego store official, trampoline park, VR/arcade and a bunch more! if you're not as intrested in shopping as other people are you may go to a desert safari in the dubai desert which you will get the chance to do: Atv's, camel riding, sand sleding, holding the falcon and after all that you get to have a buffet in the deset while watching some astonsihing fire dances.



Review On The Movies

Top Gun Maverick
Excellent film for curious aviators.

I am highly critical of acting, screenwriting, scene transitions, cinematography, special effects, music, and directing. I watch movies for entertainment, and well, to critic. There have been few movies which I call great or amazing. That's how critical I am of films. However, after I watched it, here are my thoughts: I wish there was a way for me to give this movie 10 STARS. I was almost in tears watching it and thinking "This is the best movie I've EVER seen."
